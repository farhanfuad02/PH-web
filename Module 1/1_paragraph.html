Importance of discipline

Discipline is the practice of training oneself to follow rules or a code of behavior, often through self-control and consistency. It plays a vital role in achieving personal and professional goals, as it helps individuals stay focused, manage time effectively, and overcome distractions. Whether in academics, work, or daily routines, discipline builds habits that lead to long-term success. It requires commitment and the ability to delay short-term gratification in favor of greater rewards.
